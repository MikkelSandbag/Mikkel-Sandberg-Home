FireSwing Splash Screen

FireSwing Studios was a start up that I worked at for a couple summers. This was intended to be a splash screen for the app that we were working on. Although this splash screen was never implemented, it is still one of my favorite things that I have made.

Tools Used

  • Maya

Iterations

This is the first version of the splash screen. I was still experimenting with the fluid dynamics to get the right look for the flame.

At this point, I had fine-tuned the fluid effects to get the desired look for the flames.

We wanted to try a lighter colored background.

It was requested to have a glass-breaking effect, just to see how it would look.

This is a screenshot of the final render. You can see the full animation in my demo reel on Vimeo.

About Me

My Background

I was born and raised in Nipomo, CA. I earned my Bachelor of Science in Graphic Communication from California Polytechnic State University (San Luis Obispo) in June 2015; my concentration was in Web and Digital Media.

My Interests

I enjoy coding (primarily HTML/CSS and JavaScript), playing music (trombone and bass guitar are my weapons of choice) and I also dabble as a 3D artist. You can see some of my work above.

The Future

The future is a moving target. I have a lot of passions and interests, including web development, music, 3D animation and cooking; I pursue all of these on some level, but there are some I focus on more than others.
