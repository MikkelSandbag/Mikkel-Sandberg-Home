Friend Quest (Podcast)

Friend Quest is a podcast that I do with my friends where we play tabletop role playing games. We each record our audio separately and then I edit all of the tracks in Audition to get the final cut. Then, I add the intro and outro music, record voiceovers for the intro and outro, and add some basic EQ and noise filtering. Finally, I export the final audio and bring it into Premier to create a video version of the episode as well. The cover artwork was done by Peter Freeby.

Tools Used

  • Audition
  • Premier

About Me

My Background

I was born and raised in Nipomo, CA. I earned my Bachelor of Science in Graphic Communication from California Polytechnic State University (San Luis Obispo) in June 2015; my concentration was in Web and Digital Media.

My Interests

I enjoy frontend coding (primarily HTML, CSS, and JavaScript), drinking coffee, playing video games, making video games, playing music (trombone and bass guitar are my weapons of choice), and I dabble as a 3D artist. You can see some of my work above.
